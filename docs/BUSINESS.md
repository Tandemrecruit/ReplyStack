# Business Context

## Target Market

### Primary Segment: Local Service Businesses (1-5 locations)

- Restaurants, cafes, bars
- Dental/medical/veterinary practices
- HVAC, plumbing, electrical contractors
- Salons, spas, barbershops
- Auto repair, gyms, fitness studios

### Secondary Segment: Marketing Agencies (managing 10-50 local clients)

- Higher willingness to pay
- One deal = multiple locations
- Already responsible for reputation management

## Go-to-Market (Priority Order)

1. **Cold email to marketing agencies** — One agency = many locations
2. **Local business Facebook groups** — Provide value first, soft sell
3. **Content/SEO** — Review response templates, guides
4. **Google Ads** — Test at $50-100/week after launch
5. **Product Hunt launch** — Week 3-4

## Key Risks and Mitigations

| Risk | Probability | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|------------|
| Google ships native AI responses | High | Multi-platform, agency features, voice training depth |
| Low trial conversion | Medium | Reduce friction, strong onboarding, fast time-to-value |
| High churn | Medium | Habit formation, team features, annual discounts |
